Delete your account

You're in control of your data.

You can permanently delete your H360 account and associated data at any time.

From the app

  • Open H360 and go to Profile.
  • Tap Account settings → Delete account.
  • Confirm. Your account and data are scheduled for permanent deletion.

By email

Prefer email? Send a request from your account's email address to support@h360.ai with the subject “Delete my account”. We'll verify the request and process it.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and email.
  • Your logged meals, foods, calories and macros.
  • Weight and body metrics, activity, workouts and water entries.
  • Meal and progress photos you uploaded.
  • Your goals and app preferences.

Data synced to Apple Health or Google Health Connect stays in those services — manage it from your device's Health settings.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. We complete deletion within a reasonable period (typically within 30 days), except where we're required to retain certain records to comply with law.

Questions

Email support@h360.ai and we'll help.
